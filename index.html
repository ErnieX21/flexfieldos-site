The Problem

Daily logs take 20–30 minutes

Supers are writing structured reports by hand at the end of long days. That time adds up fast across a crew.

🌧️

Weather surprises disrupt the schedule

No one has a central morning briefing on which job sites face rough conditions. Decisions get made too late.

What's Inside
Built for every part of the job.
📊

Project Dashboard

Live overview of all active projects — progress, open alerts, pending log approvals, and photos uploaded today. Everything in one place the moment you log in.

Admin & PM
🤖

AI Daily Log Generator

Type rough field notes the way a super actually talks. The AI reads them and writes a fully structured, professional log in about 10 seconds. Ready to submit or export.

AI-Powered
👷

Manpower Tracking

Log crew counts by trade and date directly in the app. Manpower data automatically flows into the daily log for that project — no re-entering anything. Supers see their site; admins see every site.

Integrates with Daily Log
🌤️

Weather Intelligence

Live weather by job site location, visible to every worker at that site. Admins get a morning briefing across all active projects so the whole team can prepare before boots hit the ground.

Live & Location-Aware
📐

Drawings

Every drawing tagged by trade and revision number. Your crew always sees the current version. Old revisions are still accessible but clearly marked as superseded — no more emailing PDFs around.

Version-Controlled
📸

Photo Gallery

Every job site photo tagged by project, trade, and date. Full-size lightbox view with uploader name and timestamp. Filter in one tap — no more digging through someone's camera roll.

Tagged & Searchable

Contracts

Send contracts for e-signature directly from the app via DocuSeal integration. Track status in real time. Full confirmation dialog before anything goes out — no accidental sends.
